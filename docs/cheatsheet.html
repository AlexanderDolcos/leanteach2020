Key:

  • ne ⇒ refers to a proof that two terms are distint.
  • ⟨⟩ ⇒ refers to fields of structure.
  • (≃) ⇒ signifies a notation we defined.
  • ^2, ^3, etc. ⇒ superscript refers to repeated arguments of the same type.
